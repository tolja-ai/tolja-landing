Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2025

Tolja Inc. ("Tolja," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website https://tolja.ai and the Tolja mobile application (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use the Service, we may collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Account credentials
  • Usage data and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To verify your identity through one-time passcodes (OTP) sent via SMS for account sign-up, login, and password reset
  • To communicate important service-related updates
  • To ensure the security of your account

3. SMS/Text Messaging

By providing your phone number during account registration, login, or password reset, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages from Tolja. These messages are limited to one-time verification codes (OTP) for account authentication purposes only.

We do not send promotional, marketing, or advertising messages via SMS. Messages are sent only in direct response to a user-initiated action. Message frequency varies based on your use of the Service. Message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For assistance, reply HELP or contact us at [email protected].

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use Twilio, Inc. as our third-party SMS delivery provider to send verification messages on our behalf. Your phone number is shared with Twilio solely for the purpose of delivering OTP messages. Twilio's use of your information is governed by their own privacy policy.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information, including your phone number, with any other third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Verification codes are temporary and expire within a short timeframe after issuance.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Temporary verification codes are automatically deleted after expiration.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at [email protected].

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.
