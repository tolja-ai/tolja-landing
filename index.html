Investment Insights Marketplace

Tolja is a marketplace platform where users can share and access trading insights and portfolio strategies.

We use transactional SMS to deliver one-time verification codes (OTP) for account sign-up, login, and password reset. No marketing or promotional SMS messages are sent.

Verify (Demo)

How It Works

1. Users create an account and verify their phone number via OTP.

2. Insights and portfolio strategies are shared within the platform.

3. Access is granted securely after authentication.

Security & Compliance

All SMS messages are strictly transactional and sent only in response to explicit user requests for authentication.

Users may opt out by replying STOP and receive assistance by replying HELP.
