SMS Verification Policy

Tolja uses transactional SMS to deliver one-time verification codes (OTP) for sign-up, login, and password reset.

Messages are sent only when you explicitly request a verification code. Message frequency depends on your account activity. Message and data rates may apply.

By requesting a verification code, you consent to receive a one-time SMS from Tolja for account authentication. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. For support: contact@tolja.ai
